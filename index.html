Quick Security Tips

1

Backup Your Passphrase

Write down your passphrase on paper and store it in a secure location. Never store it digitally.

2

Use Strong Passwords

Create unique, complex passwords for your Pi account and associated email.

3

Verify Before Clicking

Always verify links before clicking. Scammers often use URLs that look similar to official ones.

4

Stay Updated

Keep your Pi app and device operating system updated to the latest versions.

5

Be Skeptical

If something seems too good to be true (free Pi, guaranteed returns), it's likely a scam.

6

Use Official Channels

Only trust information from official Pi Network sources and verified social media accounts.
